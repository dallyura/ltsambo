How to save Excavation Data

  1. Please log in to our homepage.
    (홈페이지에 로그인 해주세요.)
  2. Please choose your current site.
    (현재 소속된 현장을 골라주세요.)
  3. Click the "Update" button and start typing.
    (Update 버튼을 눌러 입력을 시작해주세요.)
  4. Please fill up excavation data for each title.
    (각각의 타이틀에 맞는 굴착 data를 입력해주세요.)
  5. Click the "Save" button.
    (Sava 버튼을 눌러 저장해주세요.)
  6. If you want to edit or delete date, please click the "edit" or "delete" button.
    (수정이나 삭제를 원하시면 각각의 버튼을 눌러주세요.)

Why data accumulation is important?

In many industries, the value of data analysis is expending.
Cost and ability have made this an impossible area for
individuals and small businesses, but now is the time for anyone to accumulate and analyze data.
This data could be used in the future for automation,
algorithm development based on computer programming. In addition, these will contribute
significantly to the deployment of automation systems for LT SAMBO machine in the future.
